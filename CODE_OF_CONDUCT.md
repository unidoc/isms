# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) v2.1.

## Summary

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive experience for everyone. We expect all participants to be respectful, constructive, and professional.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to **conduct@isms.sh**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated.
